Varuni Sarwal

I am a senior undergraduate at the Indian Institute of Technology Delhi, where I'm majoring in Biotechnology and minoring in Computer Science. In summer 2019, I was an undergraduate researcher at The Doyle Group at Harvard SEAS advised by Professor Frank Doyle. Before this, I was an exchange student at INSA Toulouse, France supported by the Charpak Scholarship. In the summer of 2017, I was an undergraduate researcher at Zarlab, UCLA advised by Professor Eleazar Eskin and Professor Serghei Mangul.
